About Us: Óðinns Butikk


At Óðinns Butikk, we believe in making a positive impact on the world through our products. We are a company focused on creating eco-friendly bags made from sustainable materials, and we believe that style and sustainability can go hand in hand.


Our journey began when we noticed the amount of plastic waste in our oceans and the devastating effect it has on the environment. We wanted to make a difference and contribute to a greener future, so we set out to create a range of bags made from eco-materials that are not only sustainable but also stylish and functional.


Our eco-material bags are made from recycled plastics, organic cotton, and other sustainable materials that have a low environmental impact. Not only do they reduce waste, but they also look great and are perfect for everyday use. Whether you're going to work, running errands, or traveling, our bags are designed to be functional, stylish, and eco-friendly.


At Óðinns Butikk, we are inspired by the ancient Norse mythology and the god Odin. As avid lovers of mythology, we believe that the values and ideals embodied in these stories can guide us towards a better future. Just as Odin represents wisdom and the pursuit of knowledge, we strive to create products that are not only beautiful, but also serve a greater purpose for the planet.


Join us on our mission to create a more sustainable world, one eco-material bag at a time.
